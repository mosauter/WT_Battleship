Instructions

Placing Rules

  • Place a ship by clicking the place button in the place menu.
  • Type in your x value as number between 1 and 10.
  • Type in your y value as letter between A and J.
  • Your ship should be placed now. If not, your ship neither fits horizontal nor vertical in your coordinate system or ships are overlapping.
  • In this realization of battleship, ships can touch each other.
  • After placing a ship you can change the rotation of a ship by clicking the rotate button. Of course you can just rotate if the ship doesn't overlap another ship and if it fits in the coordinate system.
  • If you want to you can click the remove button to be able to set the ship again on a new position.
  • When you're happy about your placements, you have to click on I am Ready to continue.
  • You will receive a message if the server rejects one of your ships. Then you have to place your ship again. If one ship is missing in the place menu, it's already confirmed by the server and can't be changed anymore.

Fighting Rules

  • After both players are finished with placing their ships, the fight will start.
  • You can shoot on your opponents field by clicking an image in your coordinate system, on which you didn't shoot before.
  • If you hit a ship of your opponent you can shoot again.
  • If you miss, you have to wait until your opponent miss your ships.

End of game

  • If one player leaves the game, before someone has won, the other player will win automatically.
  • The game ends, if one player has hit all ships of his opponent.
  • You will receive a message and you will see where your opponent placed his ships.
